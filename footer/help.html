13. When will I receive my BlueBonus ?
The total amount you earned between August 4 and August 10 will be added to your BlueRewards account prior to August 17, and will be valid for use from August 17 through August 31. Your full BlueBonus amount must be used in a single transaction or your remaining balance will be forfeited.

To redeem your BlueBonus in store , show your unique barcode at checkout. The barcode was sent to your email.

To redeem your BlueBonus online , log into your account and your BlueBonus amount will automatically appear when you begin the checkout process.
